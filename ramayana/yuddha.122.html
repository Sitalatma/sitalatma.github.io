Valmiki Ramayana

Yuddha kanda

Book VI : Yuddha Kanda - Book Of War

chapter [Sarga] 122
Verses converted to UṭF-8, Nov 09

Introduction

Rama commands Vibhishana to honour the monkeys with gold and precious stones. When Vibhishana has honoured them with the gifts, Rama along with Lakshmana and Seetha ascend the aerial car. When the monkeys and Vibhishana request Rama to permit them to accompany in the aerial car upto Ayodhya to witness his coronation-ceremony, Rama gladly permits them to do so. All of them start on their aerial journey in Pushpaka, the wonderful aerial car which can accommodate all, spaciously.

Sloka 1

upasthitam tu taṃ kṛtvā puṣpakaṃ puṣpabhūṣitam |
avidūre sthito rāmamityuvāca vibhīṣaṇaḥ || 6-122-1

1. vihīṣhaṇaḥ = Vibhishana; kṛitvā upasthitam = keeping ready; tam = that; puṣhpakam = Pushpaka the aerial car; puṣhpabhūṣhitam = decorated with flowers; sthitaḥ = stood; avidūre = not very far; uvācha = and spoke; iti = thus; rāmam = to Rama (as follows):

Keeping ready Pushpaka the aerial car decorated with flowers, Vibhishana, for his part, standing not very far, spoke to Rama (as follows):

Sloka 2

sa tu baddhāñjalipuṭo vinīto rākṣaseśvaraḥ |
abravīttvarayopetaḥ kiṃ karomīti rāghavam || 6-122-2

2. kim karomi = "What (more) can I do?"; iti = thus; abravīt = spoke; upetaḥ tvarayā = with haste; saḥ rākṣhaseshvaraḥ = that Vibhishana; vinītaḥ = endowed with modesty; baddhāṇ^jalipuṭaḥ = and with joined palms; rāghavam = to Rama.

"What more can I do?" thus spoke with haste, Vibhishana endowed as he was with modesty and with joined palms, to Rama.

Sloka 3

tamabravīnmahātejā lakṣmaṇasyopaśṛṇvataḥ |
vimṛśya rāghavo vācamidaṃ snehapuraskṛtam || 6-122-3

3. vimṛiṣhya = after some circumspection; rāmaḥ = Rama; mahātejāḥ = of great splendor; snehapuraskṛitam = affectionately; uvācha = spoke; idam = the following words; tam = to him; lakṣhmaṇasya upashṛiṇvataḥ = while Lakshmana was listening.

After some circumspection, Rama of great splendor, affectionately spoke the following words to him, while Lakshmana was listening:

Sloka 4

kṛtaprayatnakarmāṇaḥ sarva eva vanecarāḥ |
ratnairarthaiśca vividhaiḥ saṃpūjyantāṃ vibhīṣaṇa || 6-122-4

4. sarva eva vanecharāḥ = (Let) all these monkeys and bears (ie. Denizem of forests) kṛita prayatna karmāṇaḥ = who performed tasks of exertion; sampūjyantām = be honoured; ratnaiḥ = with precious stones; vividhaiḥ arthaishcha = and riches of various kinds; vibhīṣhaṇa = O Vibhishana!

"Let all these monkeys and bears (ie. Denizens of forests), who performed tasks of exertion, be honoured with tasks of exertion, be honoured with precious stones and riches of various kinds, O Vibhishana!"

Sloka 5

sahāmībhistvayā laṅkā nirjitā rākṣaseśvara |
hṛṣṭaiḥ prāṇabhayaṃ tyaktvā saṃgrāmeṣvanivartibhiḥ || 6-122-5

5. rākṣhaseshvara = O Vibhishana the king of demons!; tvaya = by you; amībhiḥ saha = in collaboration with them; anivartibhiḥ = who never turned their backs; samgrāmeṣhu = in battles; hṛiṣhṭaiḥ = and fought joyfully; tyaktvā = ignoring; prāṇabhayam = all risk to their lives; laṇ^kā = Lanka; nirjitā = has been conquered.

"O Vibhishana the king of demons! Lanka has conquered by you, in collaboration with these monkeys, who never turned their backs in battles and fought joyfully, ignoring all risk to their lives."

Sloka 6

ta ime kṛtakarmāṇaḥ sarva eva vanaukasaḥ |
dhanaratnapradānena karmaiṣāṃ saphalaṃ kuru || 6-122-6

6. sarve eva te ime vanaukasaḥ = all these mokeys without exception; kṛitakarmāṇaḥ = have accomplished their task; kuru saphalam eṣhām karma = reward their achievement; dhanaratna pradānena = with gift of gold and precious stones.

"All these monkeys, without exceptions, have accomplished their task. Reward their achievement with gifts of gold and precious stones."

Sloka 7

evam saṃmānitāścaite nandyamānā yathā tvayā |
bhaviṣyanti kṛtajñena nirviṛtā hariyūthapāḥ || 6-122-7

7. sammanitā = highly honoured; evam = in this way; yathā nandyamānāḥ = and duly cheered; tvayā = by you; kṛitajJṇena = acknowledging their services; ete hariyūthapāḥ = the chiefs of monkeys-troops; bhaviṣhyanti = will feel; nirvṛitāḥ = happy.

"Highly honoured in this way any duly cheered by you, acknowledging their services, the chiefs of monkey-troops, will feel happy."

Sloka 8

tyāginaṃ saṃgrahītāraṃ sānukrośaṃ jitendriyam |
sarve tvāmabhigacchanti tataḥ saṃbodhayāmi te || 6-122-8

8. sarve = all; abhigachchhanti = will come nearer; tvām = to you; tyāginam = having the quality of generosity; samgrahītāram = and propitiating others; sānukrosham = showing compassion; jitendriyam = and having subjugated your senses; tataḥ = ḥence; sambodhayāmi = I am reminding; te = you.

"All will come nearer to you, for having possessed this quality of generosity, for propitiating them, for showing compassion to them and for having subjugated your senses. Hence, I am reminding you."

Sloka 9

hīnaṃ ratiguṇaiḥ sarvairabhihantāramāhave |
senā tyajati saṃvignā nṛpatiṃ taṃ nareśvara || 6-122-9

9. nareshvara = O king!; samvignā = feeling agitated; senā = the army; tyajati = deserts; tam nṛipatim = that king; hīnam = who is bereft; sarvaiḥ ratiguṇaiḥ = of all loving qualities; hantāram = and who orders them to kill people; āhave = in battle.

"O king! Feeling agitated, the army deserts that king, who is bereft of all loving qualities and who merely orders them to kill people in battle."

Sloka 10

evamuktastu rāmeṇa vānarāṃstānvibhīṣaṇaḥ |
ratnārthasaṃvibhāgena sarvānevābhyapūjayat || 6-122-10

10. evam = thus uktaḥ = spoken; rāmeṇa = by Rama; vibhīṣhaṇaḥ = Vibhishana; abhyapūjayat = honoured; sarve tān vānarān = all those monkeys; ratnartha samvibhāgena = by dispersing precious stones and gold to them.

Hearing the words of Rama, Vibhishana honoured all those monkeys, by dispersing precious stones and gold to them.

Sloka 11

tatastān pūjitān dṛṣṭvā ratnārthairhariyūthapān |
āruroha tadā rāmastadvimānamanuttamam || 6-122-11

11. tataḥ = thereafter; dṛiṣhṭvā = having seen; tān hariyūthapān = those commanders of monkey-troops; pūjitān = honoured; ratnārthaiḥ = with precious stones and gold; rāmaḥ = Rama; tadā = then; āruroha = mounted; tat = that; anuttamam = excellent; vimānam = aerial car.

Thereafter, having seen on that occasion all those commanders of monkey-troops honoured with precious stones and gold, Rama then mounted that excellent aerial car.

Sloka 12

aṅkenādāya vaidehīṃ lajjamānāṃ yaśasvinīm |
lakṣmaṇena saha bhrātrā vikrāntena dhanuṣmatā || 6-122-12

12. (ḥe mounted the aerial car); lakṣhmaṇena saha = along with Lakshmana; bhrātrā = his brother; vikrāntena = who was valiant; dhanuṣhmatā = wielding a bow; ādāya = taking; yashasvinīm = the illustrious; vaidehīm = ṣeetha; lajjamānām = who was feeling abashed; aṇ^kena = in his arms.

He mounted the aerial car along with Lakshmana, his valiant brother wielding a bow and taking the illustrious Seetha, who was feeling abashed, in his arms.

Sloka 13

abravītsa vimānasthaḥ pūjayan sarvavānarān |
sugrīvaṃ ca mahāvīryam kākutstaḥ savibhīṣaṇam || 6-122-13

13. pūjayan = bestowing his attention; sarva vānarān = on all the monkeys; savibhīṣhaṇam = as well as Vibhishana; mahāvīryam = and the mighty; sugrīvam cha = ṣugreeva; saḥ kākutthsaḥ = that Rama; vimānasthaḥ = remaining in the aerial car; abravīt = spoke (as follows):

Bestowing attention on all the monkeys as well as Vibhishana and the mighty Sugreeva, Rama remaining in the aerial car, spoke (as follows):

Sloka 14

mitrakāryam kṛtamidaṃ bhavadbhidvānararṣabhāḥ |
anujñātā mayā sarve yatheṣṭam pratigacchata || 6-122-14

14. vānararṣhabhāḥ = O the foremost of monkeys!; idam = this; mitrakāryam = work of a friend; kṛitam = has been accomplished; bhavidbhiḥ = by you; mayā anujJṇātāḥ = permitted by me; sarve = all of you; pratigachchhata = can go; yatheṣhṭam = where you will.

"O the foremost of monkeys! This work of a friend has been accomplished by you. Permitted by me, all of you can go, where you will."

Sloka 15

yattu kāryaṃ vayasyena snigdhena ca hitena ca |
kṛtaṃ sugrīva tatsarvaṃ bhavatā dharmabhīruṇā || 6-122-15
kiṣkindhāṃ pratiyāhyaśu svasainyenābhisaṃvṛtaḥ |

15. sugrīva = O ṣugreeva!; bhavatā = by you; dharma bhīruṇā = who are trembling alive to righteousness; sarvam tat = and all that; yat tu kāryam = which ought to be done; snigdhena hitena vayasyena = by an affectionate and service-minded friend; kṛitam = has been done; bhavatā = by you; yāhi = go; āshu = at once; abhisamvṛitaḥ = accompanied; svasinyena = by your army; kiṣhkindhām = to Kishkindha.

"O Sugreeva! All that which ought to be done by an affectionate and service-minded friend, has been done for your part by you, trembling alive as you are to righteousness. Accompanied by your army, proceed to Kishkindha at once."

Sloka 16

svarājye vasa laṅkāyāṃ mayā datte vibhīṣaṇa || 6-122-16
na tvāṃ dharṣayituṃ śaktāḥ sendrā api divaukasaḥ |

16. vibhīṣhana = O Vibhishana!; vasa = reside; laṇ^kāyām = in Lanka; svarājya = your own kingdom; date = conferred; mayā = by me; divaukasaḥ api = even the celestials; sendrāḥ = including Indra; na shaktāḥ = shall not be able; dharṣhayitum to attack; tvām = you.

"O Vibhishana! Settle down in your kingdom at Lanka, conferred by me. Even the celestials including Indra shall not be able to attack you."

Sloka 17

ayodhyāṃ pratiyāsyāmi rājadhānīṃ piturmama || 6-122-17
abhyanujñātumicchami sarvānāmantrayāmi vaḥ |

17. yāsyāmi = I am going; ayodhyām prati = to Ayodhya; rājadhānīm = seat of government; mama pituḥ = of my father; ichchhāmi = I bid; abhyanujJṇātum = adieu (to you); āmantrayāmi = and wish to take leave; vah = of you; sarvān = all.

" I am going to Ayodhya, the seat of government of my father. I bid adieu to you and wish to take leave of you all."

Sloka 18

evamuktāstu rāmeṇa harīndrā harayastathā || 6-122-18
ūcuḥ prāñjalayaḥ sarve rākṣasaśca vibhīṣaṇaḥ |

18. evam = thus; uktāḥ = spoken; rāmeṇa = by Rama; harīndrāh = the monkey-chiefs; tathā = and; harayaḥ = the monkeys; vibhīṣhaṇaḥ = Vibhishana; rākṣhasaḥ = the demon; sarve = and all of them there; ūchuḥ = spoke (as follows); prāṇ^jalayaḥ = with their joined palms.

Hearing the words of Rama, the monkey-chiefs, the monkeys, Vibhishana the demon and all of them there, with their joined palms, spoke (as follows):

Sloka 19

ayodhyāṃ gantumicchāmaḥ sarvān nayatu no bhavān || 6-122-19
mudyuktā vicariṣyāmo vanānyupananāni ca |

19. ichchhāmi = we (also) wish; gantum = to proceed; ayodhyām = to Ayodhya; bhavān = you; nayatu = take; sarvān naḥ = all of us there; vichariṣhyāmah = we shall range; vanāni = the gardens; upavanānicha = and groves; mudyuktāḥ = with rejoice.

"We also wish to proceed to Ayodhya. You take all of us there we shall range the gardens and groves there with rejoice."

Sloka 20

dṛṣṭvā tvāmabhiṣekārdraṃ kausalyāmabhivādya ca || 6-122-20
acirādāgamiṣyāmaḥ svagṛhānnṛpasattama |

20. nṛipasattama = O the excellent king!; dṛiṣhṭvā = seeing; tvām = you; abhiṣhekārdram = drenched with ablution (in the coronation-ceremony); abhivādya cha = and offering our salutations; kausalyām = to Kausalya; achirām āgamiṣhyāmaḥ = we shall soon return; svagṛihān = to our homes.

"O the excellent king! Seeing you, drench with ablution (in the coronation ceremony) and offering our salutations to Kausalya, we shall return to our homes."

Sloka 21

evamuktaḥ sa dharmātmā vānaraiḥ savibhīṣaṇaiḥ || 6-122-21
abravīdvānarānrāmaḥ sasugrīvavibhīṣaṇān |

21. evam = thus; uktaḥ = spoken; vānaraiḥ = by the monkeys; savibhīṣhaṇaiḥ = and Vibhishana; saḥ dharmātmā rāmaḥ = that pious minded Rama; abravīt = spoke; vānarān = to the monkeys; sasugrīva vibhīṣhaṇān = ṣugreeva and Vibhishana (as follows):

Hearing those words of the monkeys and Vibhishana, that pious-minded Rama spoke to those monkeys, Sugreeva and Vibhishana as follows:

Sloka 22

priyāatpriyataraṃ labdhaṃ yadahaṃ sasuhṛjjanaḥ || 6-122-22
sarvairbhavadbhiḥ sahitaḥ prītiṃ lapsye purīṃ gataḥ |

22. priyataram = something more dearer; priyāt = than everything dear; labdham = will be achieved (by me); yat aham gataḥ purīm = if I reach Ayodhya; sasuhṛijjanaḥ = with the host of my friends; sarvaiḥ bhavadbhiḥ sahitaḥ = and with all of you; prītim lapsye = for; I shall feel delighted.

"Something more dearer than everything dear will be achieved by me, if I reach Ayodhya with the host of my friends along with all of you, for, I shall feel delighted."

Sloka 23

kṣipramāroha sugrīva vimānaṃ vānaraiḥ saha || 6-122-23
tvamapyāroha sāmātyo rākṣasendra vibhīṣaṇa |

23. sugrīva = O ṣugreeva!; āroha = ṃount; vimānam = the aerial car; kṣhipram = quickly; vānaraiḥ saha = along with the monkeys; vibhīṣhana = O Vibhishana; rākṣhasendra = the king of demons!; tvam api ārhoha = you also ascend it; sāmātyaḥ = along with your counsellors.

"O Sugreeva! ṃount the aerial cars quickly, along with the monkeys. O Vibhishana, the king of demons! You also ascend it, along with your counsellors."

Sloka 24

tatah sa puṣpakaṃ divyaṃ sugrīvaḥ saha vānaraiḥ || 6-122-24
āruroha mudā yuktaḥ samātyaśca vibhīṣaṇaḥ |

24. tataḥ = thereupon; saḥ sugrīvaḥ = that ṣugreeva; vānaraiḥ saha = along with the monkeys; mudā yuktaḥ = gladly; āruroha = ascended; divyam = the wonderful; puṣhpakam = Pushpaka; the aerial car; vibhiṣhanashcha = as also Vibhishana; sāmātyaḥ = together with his counsellors.

Thereupon, that Sugreeva along with the monkeys gladly ascended that wonderful Pushpaka, the aerial car. Vibhishana together with his counselors also ascended it.

Sloka 25

teṣvārūḍheṣu sarveṣu kauberam paramāsanam || 6-122-25
rāghaveṇābhyanujñātamutpapāta vihāyasam |

25. teṣhu sarveṣhu = when all of them; ārūDheṣhu = ascended; paramāsanam = that excellent aerial car; kauberam = belonging to Kubera; utpapāta = flew; vihāyasam = into the sky; abhyanujJṇātam = after having been duly authorized; rāghaveṇa = by Rama.

When all of them ascended, that excellent aerial car, belonging to Kubera, flew into the sky, after having been duly authorized by Rama.

Sloka 26

khagatena mivānena haṃsayuktena bhāsvatā || 6-122-26
prahṛṣṭaścapratītaśca babhau rāmaḥ kuberavat |

26. prahṛiṣhṭashcha = feeling greatly rejoiced; pratītashcha = and satisfied; bhāsvatā vimānena = while travelling in the splendid aerial car; khagatena = which was now in the air; hamsayuktena = and was provided with the image of swan; rāmaḥ = Rama; babhau = shone; kuberavat = like Kubera.

Feeling greatly rejoiced and satisfied while travelling in that splendid aerial car, which was now in the air and was provided with the image of a swan, Rama shone like Kubera.

Sloka 27

te sarve vānararkṣaśca rākṣasāśca mahābalāḥ || 6-122-27
yathāsukhamasaṃbādhaṃ divye tasminnupāviśan |

27. te sarve = all those; mahābalāḥ = mighty; vānaraḥ = monkeys; ṛikṣhaḥ = bears; rākṣhasāshcha = and demons; upāvishan = sat; asambādham = spaciously; yathā sukham = and comfortably; divye tasmin = in that wonderful aerial car.

All those mighty monkeys, bears and demons sat comfortably and spaciously in that wonderful aerial car.
