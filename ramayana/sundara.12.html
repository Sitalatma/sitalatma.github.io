Valmiki Ramayana

Sundara kanda

Book V : Sundara Kanda - Book Of Beauty

chapter [Sarga] 12
Verses converted to UṭF-8, Nov 09

Introduction

In this chapter Hanuma gets discouraging thoughts about Seetha. He thinks that Seetha might be dead. He thus gets into depression for a moment.


Sloka 1

sa tasya madhye bhavanasya vānaro |
latā gRhāmḥ citra gṛhān niśā gṛhān |
jagāma sītām prati darśana utsuko |
na ca eva tām paśyati cāru darśanām || 5-12-1

1. saḥ mārutiḥ = ṭhat ḥanuma; darshanotsukaḥ = interested in sight; sītām prati = toward ṣeetha; madhye = in the middle of; tasya bhavanasya = that building; jagāma = went; latāgṛihān = (towards) houses made of plant creepers; chitragṛihān = art houses; nishāgṛihān = nocturnal houses; na chaiva pashyati = but did not see; tām = ḥer; chārudarshanām = with a beautiful appearance.

ṭhat ḥanuma interested in sight of ṣeetha, in the middle of that building, went towards houses made of plant creepers, art houses, and nocturnal houses but did not see Her with a beautiful appearance.

Sloka 2

sa cintayām āsa tato mahā kapiḥ |
priyām apaśyan raghu nandanasya tām |
dhruvam nu sītā mriyate yathā na me |
vicinvato darśanam eti maithilī || 5-12-2

2. saḥ mahākapiḥ = ṭhat great ḥanuma; tataḥ = then later; apashyan = not seeing; tām = that ṣeetha; priyām = dear; raghunanadanasya = to Rama; chintayāmāsa = thought thus; me = to me; vichinvataḥ = searching; maithilī = ṣeetha; yathā = in what way; darshanam = appearance; na upayiti = not being obtained(from that); sītā = ṣeetha; dhruvam = definitely; mriyate = has died.

ṭhat great ḥanuma then later not seeing that ṣeetha dear to Rama thought thus: "to me searching Seetha in whatever way appearance not being obtained, from that Seetha definitely has died."

Sloka 3

sā rākṣasānām pravareṇa bālā |
sva śīla samrakṣaṇa tat parā satī |
anena nūnam pratiduṣṭa karmaṇā |
hatā bhaved ārya pathe pare sthitā || 5-12-3

3. sā jānakī = ṭhat ṣeetha; sthitā = standing; pare āryapathe = in the best traditional path; vashīlasamrakshaṇā tatparā satī = being chaste interested in preserving her character; hatābhavet = must have been killed; anena rākshasānām pravareṇa = by the king of these Rakshasas; pratidushhṭhakarmaṇā = who performed evil deeds; nūnam = this is certain.

"ṭhat ṣeetha standing in the best traditional path being chaste interested, in preserving her character must have been killed by the king of these Rakshasas who performed evil deeds - this is certain."

Sloka 4

virūpa rūpā vikṛtā vivarcaso |
mahā ānanā dīrgha virūpa darśanāḥ |
samīkṣya sā rākṣasa rāja yoṣito |
bhayād vinaṣṭā janaka īśvara ātmajā|| 5-12-4

4. sā janakeshwara ātmajā = that daughter of king Janaka; vinashhṭā = died; bhayāt = of fear; samīkshya = seeing; rākshasa rājayoshhitaḥ = the wives of Ravana; virūparūpāḥ = with crooked appearances; vikṛitāḥ = horrible ones; vivarcha saḥ = without radiance; mahānanāḥ = with great faces; dīrgha virūpa darshanāḥ = with long and crooked eyes.

"ṭhat daughter of King Janaka died of fear seeing the wives of Ravana with crooked appearances - horrible ones without radiance with great faces with long and crooked eyes."

Sloka 5

sītām adRṣṭvā hi anavāpya pauruṣam |
vihRtya kālam saha vānaraiḥ ciram |
na me asti sugrīva samīpagā gatiḥ |
sutīkṣṇa daṇḍo balavāmḥ ca vānaraḥ || 5-12-5

5. adṛishhṭvā = without seeing; sītām = ṣeetha; anavāpya = not obtaining; paurushham = destiny; vihṛitya = and wandering; chiram kālam = for a long time; vānaraiḥ saḥ = together with Vanaras; me = to me; gatiḥ nāsthi = there is no path; sugrīva samīpagā = to go near ṣugreeva; vānaraḥ = ṣugreeva; sutīkshaṇadaṇDaḥ = has a sharp punishment; balavāmshcha = and also is strong.

"Without seeing ṣeetha, not obtaining destiny and wandering for a long time together with Vanaras, to me there is not a path to go near Sugreeva. Sugreeva has a sharp punishment and also is strong."

Sloka 6

dṛṣṭam antaḥ puram sarvam dṛṣṭvā rāvaṇa yoṣitaḥ |
na sītā dṛśyate sādhvī vṛthā jāto mama śramaḥ || 5-12-6

6. sarvam = all; antaḥpuram = inner city; dṛishhṭām = has been seen; rājayoshhitaḥ = women of Ravana; dṛishhṭāḥ = have been seen; sādhvī = the chaste; sītā = ṣeetha; na dṛishyate = has not been seen; mama shramaḥ = my effort; jātaḥ = became; vṛithā = wasteful.

"All inner city has been seen. Women of Ravana have been seen. ṭhe chaste ṣeetha has not been seen. ṃy effort became wastefull."

Sloka 7

kim nu mām vānarāḥ sarve gatam vakṣyanti samgatāḥ |
gatvā tatra tvayā vīra kim kṛtam tad vadasva naḥ || 5-12-7

7. mām = to me; gatam = who has gone back; sarve vānarāḥ = all vanaras; samgatāḥ = after meeting; kim nu = what; vakshyanti = they would say; vīrā = O strong one; gatvā = going; tatra = there; kim kṛitam = what has been done; tvayā = by you; tat vadasva = tell that; naḥ = to us.

"ṭo me who has gone back all Vanaras after meeting what they would say? O strong one! Going there, what has been done by you? Tell that to us."

Sloka 8

adṛṣṭvā kim pravakṣyāmi tām aham janaka ātmajām |
dhruvam prāyam upeṣyanti kālasya vyativartane || 5-12-8

8. adṛishhṭvā = without seeing; tam janakātmajām = that ṣeetha; kim = what; pravakshyāmi = can I say; vyativartane = due to passing; kālasya = of time; dṛivam = definitely; upaishhyanti = they will obtain; prāyam = fire.

"Without seeing that ṣeetha what can I say? Due to passing of time, definitely they will obtain fire."

Sloka 9

kim vā vakṣyati vRddhaḥ ca jāmbavān angadaḥ ca saḥ |
gatam pāram samudrasya vānarāḥ ca samāgatāḥ || 5-12-9

9. gatam = (to me) who had gone; pāram = for the other shore; samudrasya = of the ocean; kim vā = what will; vṛiddhaḥ jāmbavān = the old Jambava; vakshyati = say; saḥ = (what would)that; angadhashcha = Angada and; samāgatāḥ vānarāshcha = vanaras who meet(say).

"ṭo me who had gone for the other shore of the ocean what will the old Jambavan say? What would that Angada and vanaras who meet say?"

Sloka 10

anirvedaḥ śriyo mūlam anirvedaḥ param sukham |
anirvedo hi satatam sarva artheṣu pravartakaḥ || 5-12-10

10. airhead = non-depression; mullah = is root; shriyaḥ = of development; anirvedaḥ = Absence of despondency; param sukham = is the greatest comfort; anirvedaḥ = self reliance; satatam = is always; pravartakaḥ hi = is indeed the promoter; sarvārtheshhu = in all matters.

"ṇon-depression is root of development. Absence of despondency is the greatest comfort. ṣelf reliance always is indeed the promoter in all matters."

Sloka 11

karoti saphalam jantoḥ karma yac ca karoti saḥ |
tasmād anirveda kRtam yatnam ceṣṭe aham uttamam || 5-12-11
adṛṣṭāmḥ ca viceṣyāmi deśān rāvaṇa pālitān |

11. yat = whatever; karma = action; karoti = (human) does; tat = that; jantoḥ = (action) of man; saphalam saḥ karoti = is made to be successful by non-depression; tasmāt = for that reason; aham = I; cheshhṭe = will perform; uttamam prayatnam = a best effort; anirvedakṛitam = together with non-depression; vicheshhyām tāvat = I will search all those; deshān = regions; rāvanapālitān = ruled by Ravana; adṛishhṭān = not (yet) seen.

"Whatever action a human does that action of man is made to be successful by non-depression. For that reason I will perform a best effort together with non-depression. I will search all those regions ruled by Ravana not yet seen."

Sloka 12

āpāna śālā vicitāḥ tathā puṣpa gṛhāṇi ca || 5-12-12
citra śālāḥ ca vicitā bhūyaḥ krīḍā gṛhāṇi ca |
niṣkuṭa antara rathyāḥ ca vimānāni ca sarvaśaḥ || 5-12-13

12;13. apānashālāḥ = bars; vichitāḥ = have been searched; tathā = in the same way; pushhpagṛihānicha = flower houses; chitrashālāshcha = art houses; vichitāḥ = have been searched; bhūyaḥ = again; krīDāgṛihānicha = pleasure houses; nishhkuṭāntararathyāshcha = pathways in the middle of gardens; vimānānicha = buildings; sarvashaḥ = in all directions(have been searched).

"Bars have been searched; in the same way flower houses, art houses have been searched; again pleasure houses, pathways in the middle of gardens, buildings in all directions have been searched."

Sloka 14

iti saṃcintya bhūyo api vicetum upacakrame |
bhūmī gṛhāmḥ caitya gṛhān gṛha atigṛhakān api || 5-12-14

14. iti = thus; samchintya = thinking; upachakrame = (ḥanuma) began; vichetum = to search; bhūyopi = again; bhūmīgṛihān = undergrounds; chaityagṛihān = houses at the beginning of street intersections; gṛihāti gṛihakānapi = and also small houses faraway from the main houses.

ṭhus thinking ḥanuma began to search again undergrounds, houses at the beginning of street intersections and also small houses faraway from the main houses.

Sloka 15

utpatan nipatamḥ ca api tiṣṭhan gaccan punaḥ kvacit |
apāvṛṇvamḥ ca dvārāṇi kapāṭāni avaghaṭṭayan || 5-12-15
praviśan niṣpatamḥ ca api prapatann utpatann api |
sarvam api avakāśam sa vicacāra mahā kapiḥ || 5-12-16

15;16. saḥ mahākapiḥ = the great ḥanuma; utpatan = flying; punaḥ punaḥ = again and again; nishhpatamshchāpi = and jumping down; tishhṭhan = standing up; ghachchhan = walking; dvārāṇi apāvṛiṇvan = opening doors; kavāṭāni avaghāṭayan = pushing doors; pravishan = and entering(inside); nishhpatamshchāpi = coming out; prapatan = climbing down; utpatan api = climbing up; vichachāra = wandered; sarvam avakāsam = all opportunity.

ṭhe great ḥanuma flying again and again and jumping down, standing up, walking, opening doors, pushing doors and entering inside, coming out, climbing down, climbing up wandered all opportunity.

Sloka 17

catur angula mātro api na avakāśaḥ sa vidyate |
rāvaṇa antaḥ pure tasmin yam kapir na jagāma saḥ || 5-12-17

17. saḥ kapiḥ = that ḥanuma; tasmin ravaṇāntaḥ pure = in that city of Ravana; yam na jagāma = whatever region he did not go; saḥ = avakāshaḥ = that region; na vidhyate = was not there; chaturaṇ^guLa mātro.api = even for four angulas.

ṭhat ḥanuma in that city of Ravana - whatever region he did not go that region was not there even for four angulas.

Sloka 18

prākara antara rathyāḥ ca vedikaḥ caitya saṃśrayāḥ |
śvabhrāḥ ca puṣkariṇyaḥ ca sarvam tena avalokitam || 5-12-18

18. prakārantara radhyāshcha = streets in the middle of fort walls; vedikāḥ = platforms; chaitya samshrayāḥ = together with the intersection of four streets; dīrghikāḥ = wells; pushhkariṇyashcha = lakes; sarvam = all these; avalokitam = had been seen; tena = by him.

ṣtreets between fort walls, platforms together with the intersection of four streets, wells, lakes all these had been seen by him.

Sloka 19

rākṣasyo vividha ākārā virūpā vikṛtāḥ tathā |
dṛṣṭā hanūmatā tatra na tu sā janaka ātmajā || 5-12-19

19. rākshasyaḥ = Rakshasa women; vividhākārāḥ = of various forms; virūpāḥ = with crooked forms; tathā = and; vikṛutāḥ = horrific forms; dṛishhṭāḥ = had been seen; tatra = there; hanumatā = by ḥanuma; na tu = but not; sā janakātmajā = that ṣeetha.

Rakshasa women of various forms, with crooked forms and horrific forms had been seen there by ḥanuma but not that Seetha.

Sloka 20

rūpeṇa apratimā loke varā vidyā dhara striyaḥ |
dṛṭā hanūmatā tatra na tu rāghava nandinī || 5-12-20

20. varāḥ = the best; vidhyādhara strīyaḥ = Vidhyādara women; rūpeṇa apratimāḥ = incomparable by beauty; loke = in the world; dṛishhṭāḥ = had been seen; tatra = there; hanumatā = by ḥanuma; na tu = but not; rāghavanandinī = ṣeetha.

ṭhe best Vidhyadhara women incomparable by beauty in the world had been seen there by ḥanuma, but not Seetha.

Sloka 21

nāga kanyā vara ārohāḥ pūrṇa candra nibha ānanāḥ |
dṛṣṭā hanūmatā tatra na tu sītā sumadhyamā || 5-12-21

21.nāgakanyāḥ = nāga women; varāroha = with beautiful buttocks; pūrṇachandra nibhānanāḥ = with faces equaling full moon; dṛishhṭāḥ = had been seen; hanumatā = by ḥanuma; tatra = there; na tu = but not; sītā = ṣeetha; su madhyamā = with a beautiful waist.

ṇāga women with beautiful buttocks, with faces equaling full moon had been seen by ḥanuma there, but not Seetha with a beautiful waist.

Sloka 22

pramathya rākṣasa indreṇa nāga kanyā balādd hṛtāḥ |
dṛṣṭā hanūmatā tatra na sā janaka nandinī || 5-12-22

22. nāgakanyāḥ = nāga women; hṛitāḥ = who had been stolen; balata = forcefully; pramathya = being defeated; rākshasendreṇa = by Ravana; dṛishhṭāḥ = had been seen; tatra = there; hanumatā = by ḥanuma; na sā janakanandinī = not that ṣeetha.

ṇāga women who had been stolen forcefully, being defeated by Ravana had been seen there by ḥanuma, not that Seetha

Sloka 23

so apaśyamḥ tām mahā bāhuḥ paśyamḥ ca anyā vara striyaḥ |
viṣasāda mahā bāhur hanūmān māruta ātmajaḥ || 5-12-23

23. saḥ hanuman = ṭhat ḥanuma; mahā bāhuḥ = with great arms; dīmān = the wise one; mārutātmaja = the son of Vayu; apasyan = not seeing; tām = that ṣeetha; pasyan = seeing; anyāḥ = other; varastriyaḥ = best women; vishhasāda = became depressed; muhuḥ = again and again.

ṭhat ḥanuma with great arms, the wise one, the son of Vayu not seeing that ṣeetha and seeing other women became depressed again and again.

Sloka 24

udyogam vānara indrāṇam plavanam sāgarasya ca |
vyartham vīkṣya anila sutaḥ cintām punar upāgamat || 5-12-24

24. vīkshya = seeing; udyogam = the effort; vāṇarendrāṇām = of the best Vanaras; plavanam cha = and the crossing; sāgarasya = of ocean; vyartham = as being wasted; anilasutaḥ = ḥanuma; chintām upāgamat = got depression; punaḥ = again.

ṣeeing the effort of the best Vanaras and the crossing of ocean as being wasted, ḥanuma got depression again.

Sloka 25

avatīrya vimānāc ca hanūmān māruta ātmajaḥ |
cintām upajagāma atha śoka upahata cetanaḥ || 5-12-25

25. atha = there after; mārutātmajaḥ = the son of Vāyu; hanuman = ḥanuma; avatīrya = got down; vimānāt = from Pushpaka; upāgamat = obtained; chintām = thought; shokohahatachetanaḥ = with a mind overcome with grief.

ṭhereafter the son of Vayu ḥanuma got down from Pushpaka, obtained thought with a mind overcome with grief.
