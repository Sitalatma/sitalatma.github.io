Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam

Canto 12: The Age of Deterioration

CHAPTER SEVEN

The Purāṇic Literatures

In this chapter Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī describes the expansion of the branches of the Atharva Veda, - อถรฺว เวท, - อะถัรวะ เวทะ, enumerates the compilers of the Purāṇas - ปุราณสฺ - ปุราณัส and explains the characteristics of a Purāṇa. - ปุราณฯ - ปุราณะฯ He then lists the eighteen major Purāṇas - ปุราณสฺ - ปุราณัส and finishes his account by stating that any person who hears about these matters from someone in a proper disciplic succession will acquire spiritual potency.
